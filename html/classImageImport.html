Detailed Description

Module for matching frames with telemetry information that corresponds to them.

Reads Photographs and Video frames and creates a corresponding Frame object, adding metadata information from a telemetry file Handles image and video files as well as live video devices

Notes

Ideally this should use both the timestamp in the image's exif metadata and the time that the plane records sending the signal to take a picture plus whatever delay that exists between when the signal is sent to the camera and when the camera takes the photograph.
